description

"Eat, breathe, live biological." - It’s an initiative produced by the brand to encourage a biological culture by the citizens of Coimbra, as well as foreigners who visit the city, inserted in the culture. Our goal is to improve people's lives through a responsible, healthy and tasty diet, so we offer the most varied information about organic farming in Coimbra, such as workshops, exhibitions, conferences, unusual vegetables and the best time and conditions to plant your favorite vegetables and fruit during the year.

ABC - Biological Agriculture Coimbra (Agricultura Biológica Coimbra) is a platform born from a civil society initiative, aiming to integrate biological agriculture in the city of Coimbra. ABC’s goals are to humanize the city, promote biological agriculture and educate people to environmental matters and issues.
